Minecraft!

I guess I'll talk about Minecraft since that's on my mind right now. Minecraft is an sandbox, survival game where you can explore and build whatever you want. You can play in survival mode (with hunger, HP, etc), hardcore (difficult and only one life), or creative ("god" mode). In the base game, the main objective is to survive and beat the Ender Dragon. However, the Minecraft community has developed mods and modpacks to further expand the game and constantly bring new experiences.
Before I could afford to buy my own account, I would watch Youtubers (like DanTDM or Ssundee) play Minecraft for hours. I absolutely loved Minecraft as a kid and still do to this day. I especially love the modpacks the Minecraft community makes, since they add new life into the game. My favorite genre of modpacks are adventure and magic packs (tech modpacks are a bit too grindy for my taste). As of right now, I'm playing Mineshafts and Dungeons with my friends.
